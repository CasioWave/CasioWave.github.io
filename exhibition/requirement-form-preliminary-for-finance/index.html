Basic requirements

  • Table - per team Must be stable - exhibits CAN NOT be put at risk

  • 3 chairs (max) - per team Optional 4th for teacher - I do not prefer if as it is best if the students are unaided in the exhibition itself. But depends on what seating arrangements have been made.

  • Tablecloth - per team Essential for proper display - might seem decorative, but quite necessary.

  • Identification tags for teams Better the tags, the more organised the exhibition looks. Tags are also required because the school insignia must be covered on uniforms. Essential anti-bias measure.

  • Labels for exhibits Can be simple exhibit ID letter printouts.

  • Wall area, boards to allow posters to be set up Most projects are complimented by a set of posters, the participants must allowed space to set some up. We might recommend that they use scotch tape or something mild to stick them, as they might damage walls. In places where the posters can not be hung - provide boards with pins.

  • IMP: Electricity supply Crucial facility. Recommend 2A (? Discuss, it will at most power 2 laptops + 1 device / model). One plug per table is fine, teams can bring extension cables if they feel like they need to. Discuss with competent authority - it will be quite the undertaking to supply outlets to all teams.

  • Fans, Cooling devices Depending on the weather at the time, we may need to arrange for these. Since teams might be set up in random spaces, there might be some in places where there is no AC. They will need cooling devices - we don’t want minors having heat-strokes.

Not-very-basic requirements

  • Access to staplers, pins, tape, etc. These are supposed to be brought by the teams, but in case the teams messed up and forgot we should be able to provide some help.
  • Signage around campus to help with directions With an excess of minors on campus, it would be best to be absolutely sure that people can find their way around. (Perhaps a part of other events too? Mentioning for sake of completeness)
  • Seating area, excess chairs for guardians accompanying teams They will have to separated from the teams at the very least during the judging. We will have to make arrangements for the same.
  • Impromptu (but reasonable) requests from teams Keeping in mind the open-ended nature of it all, there might be a few outliers. There may be exhibits that require more wattage out of the outlet, some that require running water, some that require a projector screen to be hung up, etc. If considered reasonable by all involved, these should be provided.

Luxurious requirements

  • Refreshments for kids The event involves hours on hours of talking, with some exhibits being set up in areas that might be hot. Minimal refreshments should be provided.
  • Prizes While unavoidable - they will have to be given out. It would be best, if everything beforehand goes well and we have a decent amount of interest, to give out really good prizes. Discussed further in Prizes
