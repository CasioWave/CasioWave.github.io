Selection will be done by volunteers from Exhibition working group.

Most of the selection work will essentially involve weeding out low quality projects. Mostly easy to do, a few hours a week ought to do it. When we boil it down to the last few good projects, we can ask Profs for their opinions for final selections. (Reason: By that point we will hopefully have the judge panel finalised).

Important factors -

  • Create a transparent method of selection. The Exhibition can not afford to be resented in its inaugural year, of course. To do this -
    • We will provide brief feedback for every single project - least we can do after they paid us the fees. Will have to be sent out with selection results. They must justify the decision.
  • Take care in selecting group of volunteers who will dedicate time every week, in a fixed schedule, to screen submission. Slightly tedious, but amusing work. Kids are strange.

Consider asking for a fraction of the fees before selection, and asking for the rest from the selected teams only.
